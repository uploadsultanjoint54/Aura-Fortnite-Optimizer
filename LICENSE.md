# 🌟 Aura Fortnite Optimizer
 
A powerful and lightweight optimization tool built specifically for Fortnite players.
Whether you're on a low-end PC or a high-end rig, Aura helps you get the most
out of your hardware — more FPS, less lag, smoother gameplay.

---

## ⚡ What It Does

Aura applies a carefully selected set of system tweaks that target the exact
bottlenecks that hurt Fortnite performance:

- 🔥 **FPS Boost** — squeeze out every frame your hardware can deliver
- 🎯 **Input Lag Reduction** — feel the difference in your aim and response time
- 🧊 **Stutter & Lag Fix** — no more freezes at the worst moments
- ⚙️ **System Tweaks** — deep Windows optimizations for gaming
- 🧹 **Background Cleanup** — free up RAM and CPU from unnecessary processes
- 📉 **Windows Tuning** — power plans, services and priorities adjusted for performance
- 🖥️ **Windows 10 / 11** — fully supported
